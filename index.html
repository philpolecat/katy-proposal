Katy Prado is a Chilean lyric soprano and sound therapist with an international trajectory spanning over three decades. Trained at the Guildhall School of Music (London) and the University of Chile, her work bridges the gap between classical rigor and contemporary immersion[cite: 21, 26].

As a resident artist at London’s most exclusive private members' clubs for over 15 years, Katy has curated atmospheres for Soho House, The Arts Club Dover Street, and Coya[cite: 24, 58, 84]. Her versatility is evidenced by performances at major global stages including Glastonbury, the Isle of Wight Festival, and the O2 Academies[cite: 27].
